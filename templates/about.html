About Us

Bridal Union was created by the lack of guidance there is around searching for bespoke wedding dresses. There are an amazing array of boutiques where one could buy off-the-rail or something semi-bespoke (which of course is a great option), however, what if you're looking something completely unique? Something that no one else has? Something that is tailored to your every inch and need. Apart from spending ours online on google search, where else do you find a designer to do this for you? Where do you even being? Here's where we come in.

Here at Bridal Union, we bring the designers to you. We have teamed up with only the most talented proffesionals within the industry to be part of our exclusive union. You can browse through a range of designers, flick through their work and even book your first consultation with them, all in one place at the palm of your hand.

Our mission is to connect you with the right designer. We believe there is no journey quite like getting a custom made dress, especially the dress for your big day. This decision is incredibly important and we're here to guide you every step of the way.

How it works

We believe connection is important so we have designed our platform with a connection-first approach. Everything starts at our Discover Designers page where you can scroll through images posted by our designers. These are images of their work which they have done for previous brides. It's a place where they can share their works and show off their aesthetics - think instagram meet designer portfolio where you can also like and comment on their posts.

If you really really like what you seen, then you can head over to their profile (by clicking on their name on the top left corner of the image). On their profile page you can read about the designer, find out who they are, how they work, what their starting price is, view more of their work, and most importantly, book your first consultation with them.

To book a consultation you will have to be logged in, or create an account if you don't already have one. Once your account is created (or you're logged in) you can manage your bookings by going to your user account.

Our Designers
